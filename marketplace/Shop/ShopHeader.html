Get Your Red Floki!

Getting your own loveable Red Floki is easy! Simply make sure your wallet is connected and has Red Floki tokens. Next, purchase a crate and watch the magic happen as you receive one of eight random NFTs of your favorite memes. The rarer the Red Floki, the better your staking rewards and the higher you can sell them on the marketplace.

APY % is based on the price of the crate purchased. To calculate your yearly earnings simply use this formula: Crate price X (APY %/100).
